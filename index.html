繳費流程

於系統報名成功，即會跳轉至顯示選手報名細節的頁面，請依照頁面上的指示，選擇欲使用的繳費方式。若您未立即繳費也可以於「參賽名單」中，參賽者欄位的最右方點擊「付款」連結來重新登入此繳費頁面。
◇ 繳費完成後參賽者名後方的「付款」字樣會消失。各種不同的付款方式可能因為金流系統回傳資料延遲而未能即時反應。若持續一小時以上仍未反映繳費結果時請盡速與我們聯絡。

繳費期限

繳費截止期限為報名日後的第2天晚上23:59分截止，若未於時限內完成繳款者系統將自動取消未完成繳費的報名。(報名當日為第0天)
◇ 完成報名後請盡速選擇並領取繳費代碼。於繳費截止期限前一小時，就不能夠再領取任何的繳費代碼了，請特別留意！

變更報名項目

如果您在報名完成後需要變更比賽項目，最遲請於 2025/08/01 (五) 23:59 前，將報名資訊與更改的項目一併發送電子郵件至 cubingtw@gmail.com 與我們聯繫，超過截止期限就不允許變更比賽項目。

報名須知 / Registration Guidelines (English Version)

※ Please do not fill out the registration information carelessly!
If a competitor's registration information is filled out carelessly, the registration may be directly deemed invalid and removed without further notice. Any fees paid will not be refunded. Please cherish the competition slots and respect the rights of others to register.

★ Do not register multiple times to hold spots!
If you need to update the information you submitted, please use the "Contact Us" form or send an email to cubingtw@gmail.com to request modifications.
If a competitor is found to have deliberately registered multiple times, the organizers reserve the right to cancel all of that competitor’s registrations. Any fees paid will not be refunded, and the decision is at the discretion of the organizers.

※ If you already have a WCA ID, please use your WCA ID to register!
If you have previously participated in an official WCA competition and completed your competition, you will have received your own unique WCA ID after the results were uploaded.
If you are unsure about your WCA ID, you can search for your name in the "WCA Competitor List" to find it.

※ If you have registered for another WCA competition but have not yet received a WCA ID
please register for this competition as a first-time competitor. After the other competition concludes, we will actively check and update the WCA ID for first-time competitors.

★ Competition slots cannot be transferred under any circumstances!
If a competitor is unable to attend and wishes to give up their slot, they must cancel their registration before the cancellation deadline. If the official registration period has not yet closed, the canceled spot will be released and made available for public registration again. No transfer requests for competition slots will be accepted for any reason.

★ Competition materials, gifts, or prizes are available for on-site pickup only on the day of the competition. No reissuing or mailing services will be provided.
If a competitor cannot attend the competition or leaves before the award ceremony, the organizers will not provide reissuance or shipping services.
If needed, competitors may authorize friends or family to pick up items during the event period. Identity verification and signature will be required to prevent fraudulent claims.

※ 報名資料請勿隨意填寫！
若參賽者的報名資料隨意填寫，該報名將可能會直接被視為無效報名，將不另行通知直接移除！已繳納之款項也不予退還。請愛惜比賽名額並尊重其他人報名比賽的權益。

★ 請勿重複報名佔位！
如果您有需要變更您所填寫的資料，請使用「聯絡我們」或發送電子郵件至 cubingtw@gmail.com 提出修改資訊的申請。
若蓄意重複報名比賽者，主辦方將有權取消該參賽者所有報名，已繳納之款項將不予退還，由主辦方裁定。

※ 若您已經領有WCA ID，則請使用WCA ID進行報名！
如果您曾經參加過WCA認證的賽事並且有確實完成比賽者，在比賽結束成績上傳後您將會獲得一個屬於您專屬的 WCA ID！
如果您不確定您的WCA ID是什麼，那麼您可以至「WCA 選手列表」的搜尋欄位中輸入您的姓名進行查詢！

※ 若您已經報名了其他場次WCA比賽，但因故尚未取得WCA ID
請您在本場比賽以首次參賽的方式進行報名，等到其他場次的WCA比賽結束後，我們將會主動檢查並更新首次參賽的參賽者WCA ID！

★ 比賽名額不接受任何理由轉讓！
若參賽者無法參加比賽欲將名額讓出時，僅能在取消報名期限截止前，透過取消報名的辦法來取消報名，若表定開放報名時間尚未截止前，該被取消的報名名額將會釋出到開放狀態再次提供自由報名。恕不接受任何理由的名額轉讓申請。

★ 比賽提供的相關物資、贈品或獲獎獎項 僅供比賽當日現場領取，恕不提供補發、寄送服務。
若參賽者因任何原因無法於活動當日參賽，或是於頒發時先行離場，主辦方恕不提供補發與寄送的服務，若有需要領取可請親友於活動期間代為領取。為避免冒領情況，代領時需查驗身分與簽收。

首次報名參賽者注意須知 / Notice for New Competitors (English Version)

If this is your first time participating in a WCA competition, please pay special attention to the information required during registration!

First-time WCA competitors will be required to provide three basic pieces of information:
　1. Name
　2. Nationality/Region
　3. Date of Birth

Since the WCA primarily uses English names for competitor records, with native language names as supplementary information, all competitors must provide a reasonable English name.

If you will be representing Taiwan, please follow the instructions below for your English name:
★ If you already have a passport, please use the full official English name as it appears on your passport for registration.
★ If you do not yet have a passport, please use the "MOFA Bureau of Consular Affairs Chinese-English Name Translation System" to find the English translation of your Chinese name. Since the system may provide multiple romanization options, it is recommended to choose the same romanization style as other family members, if possible.

※ Special Notice: If a competitor with a Chinese name registers using an English name that is not a direct transliteration of their Chinese name, they must proactively provide a screenshot of an official document (such as a passport) and send it to the contact email (cubingtw@gmail.com) for verification. If no valid document is provided, the competition organizers will modify the English name to conform to the required format.

◇ Additional Note 1: If a competitor holds a passport that includes an "As known as" annotation, the participant may choose to use that alternative name as their official name for registration, but must provide the passport for verification.

◇ Additional Note 2: competitors with dual nationality may freely choose one nationality to represent when registering. However, all registration information must match the official documents (passport) of the selected nationality and cannot mix information from multiple documents.

Format for English Name Transliteration:

The WCA has different conventions for the romanization of names depending on the country. If you will be representing Taiwan, please adjust your English name according to the following format!
1. The given name comes first, followed by the family name.
2. If the given name has two syllables, connect them with a hyphen "-".
3. No hyphen is needed between the given name and the family name.
4. Capitalize the first letter of each syllable, with the remaining letters in lowercase.

Example: 王小名 → Xiao-Ming Wang

如果您是第一次參加WCA認證的比賽，請您特別留意報名時資料填寫的須知！

首次參加WCA比賽，會要求參賽者提供三樣基本資訊：
　1. 姓名
　2. 國籍
　3. 出生年月日

其中由於 WCA世界方塊協會 屬於國際性組織，因此對於每一位參賽者的姓名資料皆以英文姓名為主，本國語言姓名為註記，故對於參賽者提供給WCA的英文姓名有著比較嚴格的規定！

★ 若參賽者已經持有護照，請依據護照上的英文姓名，填寫至所記載的完整正式英文姓名做為報名時使用的英文姓名！

★ 若參賽者尚未持有護照，請先透過「外交部領事事務局 外文姓名中譯英系統」查詢參賽者中文姓名的英文翻譯。因為該系統會產生多種拼音結果，建議使用與家族其他成員相同的拼音形式較為恰當！

※ 特別注意：若中文姓名的參賽者於報名時填寫的非中文姓名音譯而成的英文拼音譯名時，請參賽者主動提供相關正式文件(例如護照)截圖發送至聯絡信箱(cubingtw@gmail.com)以供查驗，若未能提供有效文件查驗者，賽事方將主動替參賽者將英文姓名變更為符合格式之姓名。

◇ 額外說明1：若參賽者已持有護照且護照上有額外註記'外文別名'時，參賽者就也可用選擇該外文別名做為正式姓名，但必須要提供護照加以查驗。
◇ 額外說明2：若擁有雙重國籍時，參賽者可自由選擇代表其中一個國籍報名比賽，但資訊皆須以該國有效證件(護照)上資訊相符，不得多份證件混搭。

英文譯名的格式：
WCA世界方塊協會的紀錄上，針對不同國家參賽者的英文譯名有不同的格式習慣！請記得要先將您的英文譯名格式改寫為下方格式再進行報名哦！

格式說明：
1. 名字在前，姓氏在後
2. 若名字為兩個音節時，中間以「-」號連接
3. 姓氏與姓名之間不需要「-」連接號
4. 每個音節的首個英文字母為大寫，其餘為小寫

範例： 王小名 -> Xiao-Ming Wang
